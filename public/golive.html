🎥 Go Live on StreamFlix

Follow these simple steps to start streaming using OBS Studio. Your stream will be available to all StreamFlix viewers in real-time!

1 Install OBS Studio

Download and install OBS Studio from the official website. It's free, open-source, and available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

⬇️ Download OBS Studio

2 Configure OBS Stream Settings

Open OBS Studio and go to Settings → Stream. Select "Custom..." as the Service, and enter the following:

rtmp://YOUR-SERVER-IP:1935/live

Use any unique key. Share this key with viewers so they can watch your stream.

📋 OBS Settings Summary:

Service Custom...
Server rtmp://YOUR-SERVER-IP:1935/live
Stream Key mystream
Encoder x264 (Software)

3 Recommended Output Settings

Go to Settings → Output and configure these recommended settings for best quality:

Video Bitrate
2500-6000 Kbps
Resolution
1920×1080
Framerate
30 or 60 FPS
Audio Bitrate
160 Kbps

4 Start Streaming!

Click "Start Streaming" in OBS Studio. Your stream will automatically appear on StreamFlix! Viewers can watch by entering your stream key on the watch page.

💡 Pro Tips

  • ▸ Make sure FFmpeg is installed on the server for HLS transcoding
  • ▸ Use a wired connection for the most stable stream quality
  • ▸ Start with 2500 Kbps bitrate and increase gradually while monitoring quality
  • ▸ Add scenes and sources in OBS to make your stream visually engaging
  • ▸ Share your stream key with friends so they can join and watch!
